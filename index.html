Hello! I am Halim. I love making things, thinking deeply about user interfaces and systems, bringing people together and creating delightful products. I have been tinkering with the decentralized-protocols for some time now and have created few projects. Currently, I’m working on ZeFi. If I am not researching, writing specs for features, I will often be scrounging HackerNews, top Github repos or reading. At times, you will often find me with 100 tabs open and a notepad full of bullet notes and sketches.

I strongly believe in:

“You will get all you want in life, if you help enough other people get what they want.” by Zig Ziglar

I read a lot about many different things from philosophy to blogs on scaling distributed systems.

I like doing hackathons, design sprints, automating processes, analyzing metrics and coming up with solutions within constrainst. Over the last few years I have managed the development and launch of 3 mobile apps+ webapps with 100k+ users, consulted for blockchain startups and most sold a Decentralized Exchange on top of 0xProject and travelled and lived across 4 continents.
